The Making

For The Edit Co., carefully curated geometric elements seamlessly blend to form a harmonious composition. Each line and curve reflects the precision and attention to detail that defines the studio's approach to interior design. The clean lines signify modernity, while the subtle curves evoke a sense of fluidity and creativity. This design choice pays homage to the Art Deco era, an epoch known for its opulence, luxury, and a celebration of craftsmanship.
